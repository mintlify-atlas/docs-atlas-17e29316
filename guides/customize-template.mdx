## Overview

GitFolio offers multiple professionally designed templates to showcase your work. This guide explains how to browse, preview, and activate templates for your portfolio.

## Access templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Templates">
    From your dashboard, click **Templates** in the sidebar navigation. This opens the template gallery where you can browse all available options.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse available templates">
    The template gallery displays all templates in a grid layout. Each template card shows:

    - Template preview images (light and dark modes)
    - Template name
    - Category (e.g., Modern, Minimal, Creative)
    - Pricing information (free or premium)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Preview a template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a template to preview">
    Click on any template card to open the detailed preview view.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View template details">
    The preview page shows:

    - Full-screen template previews
    - Light and dark mode variations
    - Responsive design previews (desktop, tablet, mobile)
    - Template features and included sections
    - Pricing details
  </Step>

  <Step title="Return to gallery">
    Click **Back to Templates** to return to the template gallery and browse other options.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Activate a template

### Free templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select your template">
    From the template preview page, click the **Select Template** or **Activate** button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm activation">
    The template will be immediately applied to your portfolio. You'll see a success message confirming the change.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview your portfolio">
    Click **Preview** in the dashboard sidebar to see your portfolio with the new template.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Premium templates

<Info>
  Premium templates require a one-time purchase or active subscription depending on your account type.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a premium template">
    Click on a premium template to view its details and pricing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Purchase template">
    Click **Purchase Template** to proceed to checkout.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="One-time purchase">
        - Review pricing in USD or INR
        - Complete payment through the secure Razorpay gateway
        - Template is added to your account immediately
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="Subscription">
        Premium account holders have access to all premium templates.
        
        - Upgrade to Premium if you haven't already
        - All premium templates become available
        - Access remains active while subscription is valid
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate purchased template">
    After purchase, the template appears in your available templates. Click **Select Template** to activate it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switch between templates

You can change your active template at any time:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Templates">
    Navigate to **Templates** from your dashboard sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select new template">
    Browse and select a different template you own (free or purchased).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate">
    Click **Select Template** to make it active.

    <Warning>
      Changing templates will update how your content is displayed but won't delete any of your data. All your projects, experience, education, and skills remain intact.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Template customization options

While templates control the overall design, you can customize how your content appears:

### What you can customize

- **Content visibility** - Choose which sections to display (projects, experience, education, skills)
- **Project selection** - Select which repositories appear in your portfolio
- **Profile information** - Update bio, tagline, location, and social links
- **Skills display** - Add or remove skills shown in your portfolio
- **Color scheme** - Most templates support both light and dark modes

### What is controlled by the template

- **Layout structure** - Section arrangement and spacing
- **Typography** - Font families and sizes
- **Color palette** - Primary, secondary, and accent colors
- **Component design** - Button styles, card layouts, animations

<Info>
  Template customization happens automatically based on your content. Update your profile, projects, and other content from the respective dashboard sections.
</Info>

## Preview your portfolio

Before publishing, always preview your portfolio to see how it looks:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open preview">
    Click **Preview** in the dashboard sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review your portfolio">
    The preview shows your live portfolio with:
    - Current active template
    - All your content as it will appear to visitors
    - Responsive design for different screen sizes
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test interactions">
    - Click through project links
    - Test social media buttons
    - Verify all sections display correctly
    - Check both light and dark modes (if supported)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Portfolio URL structure

Your portfolio is accessible at:

```
https://gitfolio.com/portfolio/[your-username]
```

The template you select controls how content is rendered at this URL.

<Note>
  Your username is set during onboarding and automatically converted to lowercase. It can be updated from the **Personal Info** section of your dashboard.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Template not applying to my portfolio">
    **Solution:** 
    1. Ensure you clicked the activation button after selecting the template
    2. Check that you received a success confirmation message
    3. Clear your browser cache and reload the preview
    4. Verify your `activeTemplateId` is set in the Overview section
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Cannot purchase premium template">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Payment processing error
    - Invalid payment information
    - Currency mismatch

    **Solution:**
    1. Verify your payment details are correct
    2. Ensure your payment method supports international transactions
    3. Try a different payment method
    4. Contact support if the issue persists
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Template looks different than preview">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Missing content in certain sections
    - Browser compatibility issues
    - Cached version being displayed

    **Solution:**
    1. Ensure all required content sections are filled out
    2. Clear browser cache and hard reload (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R)
    3. Try viewing in a different browser
    4. Add content to empty sections for best results
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lost access to premium template after subscription ended">
    This is expected behavior. Premium templates require an active subscription or one-time purchase.

    **Solution:**
    - Renew your subscription to regain access to all premium templates
    - Purchase the specific template with a one-time payment
    - Switch to a free template you own
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
