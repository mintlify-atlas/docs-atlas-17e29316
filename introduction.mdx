# Welcome to GitFolio

GitFolio is a modern SaaS platform that automatically transforms your GitHub profile into a professional portfolio website. With zero coding or design effort, you get a sleek, responsive, and always-up-to-date portfolio that highlights your projects, contributions, and personal brand.

## Why GitFolio?

Your GitHub is powerful, but not always presentation-ready. GitFolio bridges that gap by creating a polished portfolio that speaks for you.

## Key features

### Professional templates

Select from curated templates including DevPro, Persona, Black & White, Clean Slate, and more. Each template is fully responsive and optimized for both desktop and mobile devices.

### Real-time portfolio rendering

Your portfolio is rendered on-demand using our dedicated rendering engine, ensuring your latest projects and updates are always visible to visitors.

### Complete customization

Manage everything through your dashboard:
- Personal information (bio, tagline, location)
- Projects with custom thumbnails and live links
- Work experience and education history
- Social media links across 8+ platforms
- Tech skills and areas of expertise

### Shareable portfolio URLs

Your portfolio is available at `portfolio.gitfolio.in/[your-username]`, making it easy to share with recruiters, clients, and collaborators.
